Chad, pictured on the left, grew up in Kansas, where he spent a lot of time on the farm and in 4H. Before Chad established Plumb Line Services LLC., he was a horse trainer. After the business started to slow down, he decided to get into construction, something he always had a natural ability at. Chad has a lovely wife, Brenda Brown, and two amazing children, Hannah and Brady Brown.

Plumb Line Services LLC. was established by Chad Brown in 2005. He had just entered the world of self-employment and was determined to work to build the business from the ground up. Now, over a decade later, he has created a business that he is proud to call his own. The main focus of his business is window and door installation, but also works in many other construction areas.
